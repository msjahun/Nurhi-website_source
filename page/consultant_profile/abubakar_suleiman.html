About Abubakar

Abubakar is a member of the board of directors and also the director of training services at Neo-Path Development Consultants. He has a bachelor’s degree in economics from Bayero University, Kano. Abubakar is a certified associate member of the Nigerian Institute of Management (NIM).

Abubakar is an academician and a training specialist with cognate experience working in a diverse socio-economic setting in Northern Nigeria.
